graph TD
    Start([Start MRD Scheduler]) --> Auth[OAuth Token]

    subgraph Search_Phase [Rate-Limited Search & Persistence]
        Auth --> SearchLimit{Rate Limiter<br/>Search API}
        SearchLimit --> SearchAPI[MG Search API]
        SearchAPI --> FluxSearch[Flux: Stream of MGIDs]
        
        %% R2DBC Persistence for Search
        FluxSearch --> R2DBC_Temp[R2DBC: Save to<br/>Search Temp Table]

    subgraph Backpressure_Buffer [Reactive Flow Control]

    end

        Buffer --> Parallel[Parallel Flux<br/>maxConcurrency]

        DetailLimit --> DetailAPI[MG Detail API]
        
        %% R2DBC Persistence for Ingestion
        DetailAPI --> R2DBC_Ingest[R2DBC: Save to<br/>Ingestion Table]
    end

    R2DBC_Ingest --> End([Process Complete])
